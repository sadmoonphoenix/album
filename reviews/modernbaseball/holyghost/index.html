This album is great. It opens up with Jake Ewald, and continues with absolutely amazing songs from him, until he hands off the rest of the album to Bren with "Coding These To Lukens". This album is just a collection of feelings, and I love it. The guitar is great, the drums are great, both frontmen really shine here, and this is a perfect album to leave Modern Baseball off with. I hope Bren is doing okay, by the way. Regardless, this album is great, and it deserves to go up in a museum somewhere. Its indie music, so it sadly never will, but one can hope.

Regardless, I give Holy Ghost a 9.8/10
